About

You can call me Van.

As a data scientist, my one giant objective is to add value.

My data science superpower is empathy, which allows me to understand problems from any domain.

I enjoy dealing with the unknown. My approach to any problem is pulling together and maximizing all types of resources and experiences.

I enjoy working with innovative, high-energy teams who are passionate about exploring new ways to solve problems.

I’m currently the Technical Excellence and Data Analytics Lead for APAC Crop Product Development at Syngenta. I use data science to exploit internal and external data sources in generating insights for the Crop Protection pipeline, which benefits both short and long-term Crop Protection business objectives.

Prior to joining Syngenta, I completed my Master’s degree in Data Science at the Asian Institute of Management, where I gained experience in delivering machine learning models and insights that are business value and impact-oriented. Having worked in manufacturing, engineering, and IT in the FMCG industry, I have had to constantly adapt with new skills.
